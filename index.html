About Me

Gerard Slee is a Principal Software Engineer at Autodesk, specializing in Extended Reality (XR) for AEC industries. With a strong architectural background and international experience in digital transformation, he bridges the gap between architecture and technology. Gerard's innovative approach in real-time visualizations drives the development of cutting-edge XR solutions, shaping the future of digital design interactions. His career embodies the synergy of creativity and technology.

My background is deeply rooted in Architecture, holding a Master of Architecture (M.Arch) degree. I leverage this understanding of physical space to build powerful digital tools. I have 8+ years of experience developing with C#, Unity, and shaders, and 5+ years running my own indie development team at Tenebris Lab.

Prior to my current roles, I spent a few years running a bootstrapped virtual reality startup developing games and software for architects and engineers. I love integrating new technologies and optimizing workflows, and I have an ability to inspire those around me.
